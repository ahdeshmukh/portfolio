ABOUT

Hi, my name is Amit Deshmukh. I am a Full Stack Software Engineer, who loves to write code. This portfolio is a quick introduction about myself. Trust me, I am much more interesting in real life.
I live with my wife and 2 goofy dogs. I enjoy spending time doing a lot of outdoor activities with my wife and dogs. Summer used to be my most favorite season, but after getting the dogs, I have started appreciating the winter as well. On a snow day, you may find us hiking along the C&O Canal, or on a hot summer day, you may find us swimming at Harpers Ferry. One of these days, I want to succeed in teaching a good game of agility to my dogs.
I graduated from George Mason Univeristy with Masters in Information Systems in 2009. Since then, I have been working as a Software Engineer, mostly using PHP. My quest to learn new skills has led me to teach myself technologies like Python and MongoDB. You will find the list of technologies that I am familiar with on my resume. Soon, I will be learning Amazon Web Services, and after that is done, I will pick something else to learn.
In me, you will find someone who is always eager to learn new technologies to add to my skill set. I believe there is no end to learning, especially in IT. You will find someone who is not afraid to make mistakes, but also someone who constantly learns from them and tries to improve oneself. If you are looking for someone perfect, or someone who memorizes theory to crack the coveted interview, I am afraid you won't find him here.
